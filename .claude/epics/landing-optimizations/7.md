# Task 7: Content Restructuring & Scannable Format

## Overview

Transform dense paragraphs into scannable, bullet-point driven content that visitors can consume quickly. Implement business-specific testimonials and visual hierarchy to improve user engagement and comprehension.

## Business Context

Users scan landing pages in 15-30 seconds before deciding to engage. Dense paragraphs reduce comprehension by 40% compared to bullet points. Visual hierarchy and business-specific social proof increase trust and conversion rates significantly.

### Mobile-First Content Design

#### Content Adaptation Rules
- **Desktop:** Full testimonials with photos
- **Tablet:** Condensed testimonials, maintain bullet points
- **Mobile:** Testimonial quotes only, icon-based bullets

#### Reading Experience Optimization
- Line height: 1.6 for body text
- Paragraph spacing: 1.5rem
- Maximum line length: 70 characters
- Sufficient color contrast (4.5:1 minimum)

## Content Guidelines

### Writing Style
- **Active voice:** "Automatiza citas" not "Las citas son automatizadas"
- **Specific metrics:** "Reduce 60%" not "Reduce significativamente"
- **Action-oriented:** "Implementa en 24h" not "Disponible para implementar"
- **Benefit-focused:** "Ahorra 10 horas/semana" not "Sistema eficiente"

### Tone & Voice
- Professional but approachable
- Solution-focused, not problem-heavy
- Confident without being pushy
- Mexican business context awareness
